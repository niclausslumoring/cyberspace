Makeblock

Our Makeblock program offers students an immersive experience in the realms of Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts, and Mathematics (STEAM). Through hands-on projects, students delve into the fascinating world of robotics, coding, and STEAM education. This program enables students to develop a solid foundation in problem-solving, logical thinking, and teamwork while honing their technical skills.

MakeX Competition

Make X is an exciting program designed to ignite students' passion for coding and robotics. Participants will have the opportunity to engage in thrilling robot competitions, putting their skills and creativity to the test. By building and programming their own robots, students develop a deep understanding of coding principles, engineering concepts, and the invaluable ability to think on their feet in high-pressure situations.

Kidspreneurship

Kidspreneurship is a term that combines "kids" and "entrepreneurship" and refers to the practice of teaching entrepreneurship skills and mindset to children. It involves introducing young children to the concepts of business, innovation, problem-solving, creativity, and financial literacy. Kidspreneurship programs aim to foster an entrepreneurial mindset in children, encouraging them to think critically, take risks, identify opportunities, and develop skills such as communication, teamwork, and leadership.

Taekwondo

Taekwondo Program: Embark on a journey into the art of Taekwondo through our professionally guided program. This class prioritizes physical fitness, discipline, and self-defense, providing a comprehensive approach to martial arts learning. Focusing on the development of coordination, flexibility, and strength, our program goes beyond traditional martial arts settings. Students can expect improved physical fitness, enhanced self-discipline, practical self-defense skills, and ample opportunities for leadership and personal growth.

DJ Class

Explore the exciting realm of DJing with our specialized program. Here, we concentrate solely on teaching the art and techniques of DJing, without any references to coding, robotics, or competitions. Covering everything from equipment operation and music theory to beatmatching and live performances, students gain hands-on experience with professional DJ gear. This unique program provides a perfect blend of musical creativity, technical skill development, confidence-building through live performances, and exposure to diverse music genres and DJ styles.
